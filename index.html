About Me

I was born in Dalian, China and raised in Auckland, New Zealand — two environments that taught me to adapt, stay curious, and connect across cultures. That mindset has shaped how I lead and how I solve problems.

My passion for business began in high school when I founded a nonprofit that turned children’s art into postcards to raise funds for autism support programs. That project taught me how creativity, empathy, and business can work together to create impact.

At NYU Stern, I study Finance and Data Science. I’ve interned at J.P. Morgan and Morgan Stanley, contributing to multi-billion dollar M&A and credit deals. My goal is to help purpose-driven companies scale with both precision and heart.
